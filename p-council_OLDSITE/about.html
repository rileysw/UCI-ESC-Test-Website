About P-Council

WHAT IS P-COUNCIL?


  1. Engineering Student Organization Presidents’ Council is to serve as an open forum to collaborate on ideas, suggestions, complaints, and grievances of the students and student-run engineering and ICS organizations of the Henry Samueli School of Engineering and Donald Bren School of Information and Computer Sciences. This forum and council is overseen by the Vice President of Internal Affairs of ESC.

  2. All legislative authority of ESC shall be vested in the ESO President's’ Council, which shall be the supreme policy-making body responsible for the development of policies pertaining to ESOs or the engineering student body at large, and shall promote the welfare and the interests of the members of ESC.

WHY SHOULD YOU JOIN?


BESO

  • Compete in Battle of the ESOs! These are friendly competitions any ESO can host throughout the year. We’ve done a dodgeball tournament, ultimate frisbee game, video game tournament, board game nights, blood drive, and more!
  • BESOs give us an opportunity to interact, socialize, and meet other ESOs and its members. They help strengthen relationships within each ESO because each ESO is aiming to be the BESO Champions of the year. It’s pretty much a way to have some fun with other ESOs.
