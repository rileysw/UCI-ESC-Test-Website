Engineers Mentoring the Future

  2. Engineers Mentoring the Future

Engineers Mentoring the Future (EMF) pairs first-year mentees with upper-class student mentors. This program provides valuable information and guidance to young engineers, both mentees and mentors, through events such as course-planning sessions, resume workshops, and mock interviews.
