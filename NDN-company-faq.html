What if my company cannot attend the NDN anymore?

If you would like to cancel your reservation for Networking Dinner Night, please contact engitech+ndn@uci.edu for further arrangements. Any cancellations 2 weeks before NDN will result in a $250.00 non-refundable fee to cover the cost of table.



We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, all personal information will not be disclosed to individuals outside the designated ESC corporate affairs committee.
