The Corporate Affairs Committee is dedicated to promoting professional development for the engineering community by providing networking opportunities and career fairs to connect students and industry representatives.

The committee aims to organize professional events that are accessible to all students. In addition to planning a summer professional development workshop series and industry information sessions, the committee organizes Networking Virtual Night and EngiTech Career Fair to help students engage with employers.
