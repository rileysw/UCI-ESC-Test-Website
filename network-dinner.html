About Networking Dinner Night

Networking Dinner Night is an event that enables students and company representatives to interact in a relaxed yet professional environment. Representatives will share personal experiences related to their companies and recruit from qualified engineering students. Students will have the opportunity to ask questions and gain a deeper understanding of the engineering industry.
