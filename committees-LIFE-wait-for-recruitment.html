Leaders in Freshman Engineering

The Leaders in Freshman Engineering is composed of first-year undergraduate engineering students. Members collaborate to host events that engage faculty and students in the the school of engineering. Through this program, members gain leadership experience and skills valuable to a strong engineering foundation.
