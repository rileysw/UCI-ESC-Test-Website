About Engineering Student Council

Engineering Student Council (ESC) is a non-profit, student-run organization established to represent UC Irvine's engineering student body! Our goal is to serve as the central network of communication and support between students, alumni, faculty, and industry professionals, in order to facilitate a culture of successful and innovative engineering. Through collaboration with all members of ESC, we strive to support UCI's engineering community while promoting personal and team development.

ESC's structure consists of our Executive Board (President and 6 Vice Presidents), and our seven core Committees (Community Outreach, Communications, Corporate Affairs, Engineers Mentoring the Future, Faculty Engagement, Leaders in Freshman Engineering, and Student Involvement).

Our committees work hard to provide students resources, professional development workshops, socials, faculty and student networking events, mentorship programs, career fairs, and more. To learn more about them, check out the committees tab at the top of the page.
