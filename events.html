About Events

National Engineers Week (E-Week)

Held in February, E-Week calls attention to the state of the art achievements in engineering. Engineering students, faculty, and professionals are encouraged to attend professional and social events, hosted by ESC and other engineeering student organizations on campus, during this weeklong celebration.

Networking Dinner Night

The Networking Dinner Night is an event hosted by the Engineering Student Council. It enables company representatives and students to interact in an intimate yet professional environment. In this relaxed setting, representatives can share personal experiences related to their company and recruit from qualified STEM students. Students will have the opportunity to ask questions and gain a deeper understanding in how STEM companies thrive. Click to learn more.

Art of Engineering

Art of Engineering showcases the amazing talent we have in engineering. Individual and group performances take center stage to kick off the new school year. The audience is treated to a night of dancing, singing, and entertainment, and even a chance to win fun prizes!

E-Ball

E-ball is hosted by ESC. Spend the evening dancing, chatting, and enjoying hors d'oeuvres underneath the California sky. E-Ball commemorates a year of engineering coursework, and the friendships and laughter shared along the way.
