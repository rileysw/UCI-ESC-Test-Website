E-Week 2020

National Engineers Week, known colloquially as E-Week, is a weeklong celebration of engineers. Join us February 24 to February 27, 2020 for a week of exciting social and professional events hosted by ESC and other student engineering organizations on campus.



Kickoff Fair To kick off E-Week 2020, organizations on campus host exciting activities in the spirit of engineering. This event is open to all students and faculty, as anyone can appreciate the wonders of engineering.
Lip Dub Leaders in Freshman Engineering (LIFE) choreograph an energetic and easy-to-learn dance to a popular song. Join the flashmob at the end and have a chance to be featured on our YouTube channel. Learn the dance, join the fun!
Dean's Breakfast Come hang out with your favorite professors as they cook you breakfast! Yep, you read that right. Engineering Faculty members will be making delicious pancakes, and engineering students will enjoy a complimentary breakfast!
EngiTECH Career Fair Land your dream job or internship at our annual EngiTECH career fair! Companies look to recruit outstanding engineering & ICS students.
E-Week BBQ Students can enjoy a complimentary BBQ burger lunch. Engineering & ICS students are welcome!
Shadow Day High school students shadow current engineering students to learn more about engineering and higher education.
Awards Banquet Outstanding professors, TAs, and students from each engineering department will be honored at this annual Engineering Awards Banquet.
Faculty Sports Day Faculty, alumni, and students partake in this annual friendly competition.
