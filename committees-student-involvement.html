Student Involvement Committee

The Student Involvement Committee is responsible for organizing ESC socials, inter- organization socials, and fundraisers. This committee also organizes sporting events such as the Student-Faculty Softball game, and the E-Week Competitions. Committee members should be organized, enthusiastic, and have a desire to foster greater student to student connections within the engineering community.
