FAQ



Be on Time

We will have company representatives there that can potentially hire you or give you an internship, do not give off a bad first impression.


Be Yourself

Show companies who you really are, do not try to lie and pretend to be someone you are not.


Have Questions in Mind

Be prepared to ask questions that are deeper than, “What does your company do?”.


Research the companies! It always looks good to know about the company.



How can I prepare for NDN?


ESC will host a professional etiquette session on Wednesday, Oct. 10, 2018 to help prepare for NDN.

If you cannot make it to one of our events, make sure to look at some youtube videos and try practicing at home.



What is the dress code?


Business Casual, we want you to feel relaxed and comfortable the day of the event, but we must still remember that you will be meeting company representatives. (Ex. Polo, Blouse, Button Up, Pants, Cardigan)



Can I change a dining option that I made in the survey?


Please email engitech+ndn@uci.edu with your name and your new choice of dinner.

Subject your email “DINING, *first and last name*”.



I cannot come to NDN but I already bought my ticket.


No worries, just email engitech+ndn@uci.edu
