About EngiTECH Career Fair

ESC is proud to host the 46th annual EngiTECH Career Fair! If you don't know what EngiTECH is, it's a great chance for you to network and possibly pick up an internship or job! This event caters to both Engineering and ICS majors on campus and is part of a series of events we're having for E-Week!
