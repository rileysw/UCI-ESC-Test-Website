About Networking Dinner Night

The Networking Dinner Night is an event hosted by the Engineering Student Council. It enables company representatives and students to interact in an intimate yet professional environment. In this relaxed setting, representatives can share personal experiences related to their company and recruit from qualified STEM students. Students will have the opportunity to ask questions and gain a deeper understanding in how STEM companies thrive.

About Engineering Student Council

Networking Dinner Night is hosted by the Engineering Student Council (ESC), a non-profit, student-run organization dedicated to enhancing the university experience of many leaders and scholars in the engineering community. Our main goal is to bridge the gaps between students, alumni, faculty, and industry professionals. We do this by cultivating relationships through purposeful events such as NDN, Engitech, E-Week and many more.
