About Us

Engineering Student Council (ESC) is a non-profit, student-run organization established to represent UC Irvine's engineering student body! Our goal is to serve as the central network of communication and support between students, alumni, faculty, and industry professionals, in order to facilitate a culture of successful and innovative engineering. Through collaboration with all members of ESC, we strive to support UCI's engineering community while promoting personal and team development. 

ESC Committees Structure

ESC consists of our Executive Board (President and 5 Vice Presidents) and seven Committees (Community Outreach, Corporate Affairs, Faculty Engagement, Student Involvement, Communications, Leaders in Freshman Engineering, and Engineers Mentoring the Future).

Our committees work hard to provide students resources like professional development workshops, faculty and student networking events, socials, mentorship programs, career fairs, and more. To learn more, check out the Committees tab at the top of the page.
