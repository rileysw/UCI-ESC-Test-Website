ABOUT US ROOM RESERVATIONS

We serve as an open forum for collaboration between students and student-run engineering and ICS organizations.

Engineering Student Organization Presidentsâ€™ Council serves as an open forum to collaborate on ideas, suggestions, and concerns of students and student-run engineering and ICS organizations of the Henry Samueli School of Engineering and Donald Bren School of Information and Computer Sciences. This forum and council is overseen by the Vice President of Relations of ESC.

ESO's President's Council has legislative authority over Engineering Student Council. P-Council is responsible for policy-making, specifically the development of polices pertaining to ESOs or the engineering student body at large. P-Counil promotes the welfare and the interests of the members of ESC.

Get Funding

We apply for and obtain annual funding from the Dean's office. Funds are dispersed among Engineering Student Organizations who take part in P-council.

Stay Connected

Attending P-Council meetings offer a chance for members of different clubs to come together each week and stay connected with other organizations in our community.

Collaboration

We are an open forum to collaborate on ideas, suggestions, complaints, and grievances of the students and student-run engineering and ICS organizations
